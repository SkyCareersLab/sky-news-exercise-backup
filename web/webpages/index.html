Warning: 'Cool Selfie Could Cost Your Life'

A campaign uses road sign-style warning signs to highlight the dangers when taking personal photos in hazardous scenarios. Some 10 people have been killed and 100 hurt already this year in accidents while snapping hazardous images, from posing with a loaded gun or a hand grenade to climbing on to a railway bridge.
